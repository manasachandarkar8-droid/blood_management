How Emergency Timers Work
Emergency requests automatically escalate based on time remaining to deadline. Critical (<1h or expired) • Urgent (<4h) • Moderate (<12h) • Stable (>12h). Critical and Urgent requests appear in the Dashboard Critical panel automatically.
